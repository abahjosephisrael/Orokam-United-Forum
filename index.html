About Us

The idea of (OUF) was a concept by James Echeofun Abutu, which was supported by Late Chief A. A Igoche, Pastor (Mrs) Comfort Luwei and our brother Augustine Favour Itodo who gave me the synopsis to maintain the idea. For this great vision we are seeing here today.

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

The aims of this forum are to bring peace, unity, progress, development to Orokam.

To unite all the five clans in our community to bring 21st century change and development to promptly cater for all the five clans in our father land.

To produce good and quality leaders who will be useful in developing this community.
To achieve numerous capital and programmed development projects like pipe borne water,model basic school and other great impactful developments to move the communities into a more developed society of 21st century.
To unite our members at home and diaspora who are not close to the community and those who have lost contact with their indigenous brothers and sisters.
To cater for the unity, progress, growth, stability, peace, cultural, socio-economic and political land scale of these great (OROKAM) communities.
To make sure that Individuals who are far and near from the communities provide information on the activities and situational patterns of communities members that have done or provide a potential progress.

Finally, i want to appreciate all other indigenous groups from these communities for prompt and optimal support, following up this golden development created for our community.
